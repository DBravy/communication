About Puzzle Solving Training

This mode trains the communication system specifically for puzzle solving - learning to transform input grids to output grids by understanding ARC puzzle rules.

  • Training Setup: Multiple puzzles with training and test examples
  • Goal: Learn general transformation patterns from demonstrations
  • Architecture: Encoder extracts rules from training examples, applies them to test inputs
  • Communication: Agents communicate about puzzle rules and transformations

Note: This is different from the main training page - here we train on puzzle solving as the primary task (TASK_TYPE = puzzle_solving).
