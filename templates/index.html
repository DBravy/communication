Training Pipeline Overview

Step 1: Pretraining (Optional) - Train the encoder alone on a supervised task:

• Binary Classification: Distinguish real ARC grids from random noise

• Selection Task: Select correct grid from distractors

• Puzzle Classification: Classify grids by puzzle ID (inputs/outputs separated)

• Optional: Continue from a previous checkpoint

Step 2: Main Training - Train the full communication system (sender + receiver):

• Load Pretrained Encoder: Initialize with encoder from Step 1 (if available)

• Freeze Encoder: Keep pretrained encoder weights fixed (recommended)
